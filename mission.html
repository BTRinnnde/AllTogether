Our Mission

All Together is a "virtual artist" that creates relaxing, situational music—piano, jazz, white noise, and more—designed to fit seamlessly into your daily life. But here's what makes us different: every cent of streaming revenue goes directly to people and causes in need.

Think of it as a musical donation button. You press play, the streams add up, and the money flows straight to good causes. No cuts, no overhead, no hidden fees—just effortless giving through music you actually want to listen to.

We believe in complete transparency. You'll see exact stream counts, earnings, and proof-of-payment receipts for every donation. Our goal is to make charity as simple as hitting play, while building a community that follows our impact and feels ownership in this mission.

Together, we're turning passive listening into active giving. Every stream matters, and every listener becomes part of something meaningful.
