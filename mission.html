ALL TOGETHER

Our Mission

All Together was created to offer a new way for people to contribute to causes that matter and need funding. Our mission is simple: we take 100% of the royalties earned from music streams and give them directly to someone in need.

We believe in complete transparency—nothing will be hidden from you. We'll provide regular updates on streams, income, and exactly where the money goes.

Our vision is to provide music that fits any situation you find yourself in. This way, you're not just supporting a good cause—you're also enjoying the music you play. Together, we can make a meaningful impact simply by listening.
